Our Story

LittleLamp was created by a parent who believed that every child deserves beautiful, engaging Bible stories they can actually understand and enjoy.

Our mission is simple: to make God's Word come alive for children aged 3 to 10 through stunning illustrations, warm audio narration, and stories told with care and accuracy.

Every story in LittleLamp is illustrated on demand using AI-assisted art, narrated in a gentle voice, and built around a lesson and a prayer — so each story becomes a complete devotional experience for the whole family.

LittleLamp is and always will be completely free. We believe every child, in every home, should have access to God's Word in a form that delights them.

Thank you for trusting us with your little ones.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

LittleLamp does not collect personal data from children. We do not require account creation to read stories. The only personal information we may collect is an email address if you choose to subscribe to the Story of the Week newsletter.

How we use it

Email addresses are used solely to send the weekly story newsletter. We never sell, share, or rent your information to third parties.

Third-party services

LittleLamp uses third-party technology services to power its audio narration and story illustrations. These services process content prompts only and do not receive any personal user data.

Children’s privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy. No data is collected from children under 13. Parents or guardians with questions may contact us at hello@littlelamp.app.
