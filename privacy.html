LittleLamp Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

About LittleLamp

LittleLamp is a free Bible storybook app for children aged 3-10, featuring 100 illustrated stories with audio narration, moral lessons, and prayers.

Information We Do Not Collect

LittleLamp does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. We do not require account creation, registration, or login of any kind. No personal data is collected from children or adults using this app.

No Ads

LittleLamp does not display advertisements and does not work with any advertising networks.

Audio Narration

LittleLamp uses the ElevenLabs text-to-speech service to generate audio narration. Story text is sent to ElevenLabs solely for the purpose of generating audio. No personal user data is shared with ElevenLabs.

Images

All story illustrations are hosted on our servers and loaded when you view a story. No user data is transmitted when images are loaded.

Children's Privacy

LittleLamp is designed for children and takes children's privacy seriously. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app functions fully without any user data collection.

Third-Party Services

LittleLamp uses the following third-party services:

  • ElevenLabs - for audio narration generation (text only, no personal data)
  • Bunny CDN - for fast image and audio delivery
  • GitHub Pages - for app hosting

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
